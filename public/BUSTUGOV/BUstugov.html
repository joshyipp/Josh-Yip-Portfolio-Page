About Us

At a university that is extremely vigilant of the inclusivity, enthusiasm, and energy it can breed, unifying the student body is a spirited goal of BU Student Government. More importantly, we emphasize affordability, safety, and coordination of our events. This year, BU will host our very own TerrierFest. Given the unique nature of this event and its ability to bring together thousands of students, we work hard to ensure everyone is happy to be at BU.

Reshaping the Culture

This year, Student Government takes on an initiative to redefine the BU community and culture. With more than 450 clubs and organizations, our goal is to intertwine the originality and identity our students embody. From Artist Concerts, DJ Stages, Food Fests, Mixers, and Student Performances, we will create a new culture filled with high quality entertainment, exclusive to our community.

Making Waves with Culture, Defining our Strategy, and Delivering New Experiences.
